See Katherine McNamara Show Off Her Legs In A Sexy Peach Dress

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Katherine McNamara looks stunning showing off her long legs in a peach dress.

By Mark McKee | Published 1 year ago

“I may be in distress, but don’t call me a damsel.” These are the words uttered by the one and only Katherine McNamara in a post on her Instagram, displaying that she is a strong woman playing strong characters. And playing strong characters is precisely what she does with her characters in Arrow, Walker: Independence, and The Maze Runner, but as you can see from the most recent post on her Instagram, that doesn’t keep her from embracing her feminine energy. 

The post contains two images of the Arrow actress posing in a long peach dress with a tall slit and a strapless corset top. The first image sees her posing in a kneeling position, while the second is a more candid shot of her sitting on a stool and laughing as the photographer catches her in a moment of joy. Her caption is a fitting french phrase meaning the jovial enjoyment of life. 

Of course, Katherine McNamara wouldn’t post something without calling out those who made the shoot possible. Involved in getting her look together are a celebrity stylist, Jordan Gross, make-up artist Kendal Fedail, hair stylist Maddie Torres, and the magic behind the camera, Shintani. The team came together to bring a gorgeous shot, of course, with a canvas like Katherine McNamara; anyone could be an artist. 

Jordan Gross is a George Washington University educated and Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising trained stylist located in Los Angeles. She previously worked with massive names such as Karen Gillan (Dr. Who and Guardians of the Galaxy) for Foxes, Alison Brie (Glow and Community), and Karen Fukuhara (Bullet Train and The Boys). The shoot in the peach dress isn’t the first time she worked with Katherine McNamara, as she is a part of her extensive portfolio on her website. 

Kendal Fedail is a Los Angeles-based make-up artist trained at the Make-Up Designory in California; she also supplements her time working for celebrities by working as a make-up artist at Nordstrom (according to her LinkedIn). She has worked with Madison Bailey (Outer Banks and Black Lightning), Emma Kenney (Shameless and The Connors), and Nicola Peltz Beckham (Transformers: Age of Extinction and The Last Airbender). 

Maddie Torres has worked with Katherine McNamara before, as she pops up earlier on her Instagram, but spends the majority of her time as a stylist studying under LA salon owner Andy LeCompte. She has also worked with Kim Kardashian. Shintani is a multifaceted LA-based artist with an extensive photography portfolio and the perfect fit for someone like Kat. 

Katherine McNamara is most well known for her roles as the daughter of Oliver Queen and Felicity Smoke on Arrow, Clary Fray on Shadowhunters, and her most recent role as Abby Walker in the Walker: Texas Ranger prequel, Walker: Independence. But her ability to capture the attention of anyone lucky enough to lay their eyes on her modeling endeavors shows that she is much more than a talented actress. The camera loves her nearly as much as the fans watching her.
