Biography

Hank Willis Thomas was born in Planfield, NJ on March 17, 1976. The conceptual artist is known for his work in sculpting and focuses on themes relating to perspective, identity, and culture. His work is in the permanent collections of several major museums including the Museum of Modern Art in New York and the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum. He shared a portrait he took of Kiran Gandhi to his Instagram in November of 2018. He earned his BFS in 1998 from NYU. He then earned his MA in 2004 from the California College of the Arts where he also earned his MFA in Photography. He later received an honorary Ph.D. from the Institute of Doctoral Studies in Visual Arts and from the Maryland Art Institute.
