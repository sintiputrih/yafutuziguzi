Biography:

Lina Esco was born on May 14, 1985 in Miami, Florida, USA. In 2006, she was cast in her first guest role, playing the character Angie Watson in CSI: NY. In the 2010s, Esco has had performances in a couple of movies, which include LOL and Fairy Dust. She also ventured into filmmaking with the movie she wrote and directed, Free the Nipple. After recurring roles in Flaked and Kingdom, Esco began starring as Christina “Chris” Alonso in S.W.A.T.
