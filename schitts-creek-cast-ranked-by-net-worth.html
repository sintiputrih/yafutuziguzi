The Cast Of Schitt's Creek Ranked By Net Worth

September 2024 · 9 minute read

Highlights

  • The Schitt's Creek cast net worths range, from Annie Murphy nearly broke before getting her role on Schitt's Creek , to Chris Elliott with a surprising $10 million.
  • Some, like Emily Hampshire and Jennifer Robertson, earned millions post- Schitt's Creek while others became more well-known.
  • Dan Levy, with a $14 million net worth, made his mark on and off-screen in the successful series.

The cast of Schitt's Creek ranges from acting legends such as Catherine O'Hara and Eugene Levy to (then) newcomers like Annie Murphy who was nearly broke at the time she was cast on the series. Thankfully, Schitt's Creek became a success and lasted six seasons, so Murphy is no longer broke. She also received an Emmy Award in 2020 for her work on the show. No doubt the success of the series added to each of the actor's net worths, but which of the cast members has the most money today?

Fans may be surprised to know just how much the actor who plays Roland is worth, for instance, while they may not be as shocked about O'Hara's earnings. The cast also includes lesser-known stars such as Emily Hampshire and Jennifer Robertson, who have had other roles, of course, but how much are they worth now?
