Who is Wilfried Mbappe?

Wilfried Mbappe is a French-Cameroonian citizen of African descent. He was born in Douala, Cameroon, before moving to France. His date of birth is the 11th of October, 1970, which makes him 53 years of age as of 2023.

He started his educational journey in Cameroon, attending a local elementary school for his primary and secondary schooling before moving to Paris, France to get his college degree in Physical Education.

Wilfried made a permanent move to France and settled in Bondy, which is close to Paris. He tied the knot and is now the father of two sons, Kylian and Ethan, and an adopted son, Jires Kombo Ekoko.

Career

Wilfried Mbappé pursued his ambition to be a footballer by moving from Cameroon to France in order to play for teams in the country’s regional leagues. Unfortunately, he wasn’t given the opportunity to join some of the premier teams in France. Mbappé did not let this setback affect him.

He established himself in Bondy, which is a Paris suburb, and that is where he took on the managerial aspect of football. As a coach, he earned a great deal of respect due to his work with young, talented players, helping them enhance and develop their abilities. He became part of AS Bondy, one of the well-known clubs in the area.

The football coach spent 25 years at AS Bondy, acting in a variety of roles. He was the sporting director for the 10-17 age range, and through his talent for recognizing ability, he mentored famous players such as Arsenal’s William Saliba and Fiorentina’s Jonathan Ikone.

Once he had left AS Bondy, Mbappé became a football representative. He devoted his energy to helping his firstborn son, Kylian Mbappé, cultivate his passion for the sport. This led to the young footballer being enrolled at Bondy at an early age. The training of his son and his enrollment at AS Bondy is now referred to as “Project Mbappé” worldwide.

Hence, it is no wonder that Kylian Mbappe has shattered many records in the sports industry and he is only just beginning. His most recent accomplishment that had people around the globe elated was when he established the record for the highest number of goals at the World Cup final and obtained the Golden Boot and Silver Ball for the 2022 FIFA World Cup.

Wilfried Mbappé is an accomplished individual who once played football and coached, and at present is an agent, and television personality.

Personal Life

Wilfried Mbappé tied the knot with Fayza Lamari, a former professional handball player and current media celebrity who was born in France in 1974. Fayza represented her native Algeria in the sport before embarking upon a successful career in television in her home country.

Mbappé and his long-term spouse tied the knot in 1997, and they have two sons; Kylian and Ethan. Additionally, they adopted Jires Kembo Ekoko, who was a friend of Mbappé’s who had passed away.
