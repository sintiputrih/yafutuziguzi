Jami Gertz Net Worth: Business &amp; Charity

February 2024 · 9 minute read

American Entrepreneur, actress, and venture capitalist Jami Beth Gertz, known as Jami Gertz in showbiz, net worth is about $3 billion.

Jami has appeared in numerous television series, best known for her roles in movies such as Crossroads, The Lost Boys, Less Than Zero, and Quicksilver.

Her notable appearances in television series include Square Pegs, Twister,  CBS sitcom Still Standing, and 2012-2014 ABC sitcom The Neighbors.

She made her acting debut in the early 80s with an on-screen role in the 1981 film On the Right Track.

Apart from appearing in some notable movies and television series, Jami is also a part-owner of Atlanta Hawk of the National Basketball Association (NBA).

Gertz married investor Antony Ressler in 1989. Antony Ressler is a venture capitalist who has invested in several multi-billion dollars projects.

Soon after the couple was married, Antony co-founded the private equity firm Apollo Global Management followed by Ares Management in 1997.

Both of these companies have multi-billion dollars assets under management. Ressler owns an approximated 33% of the firm’s share.

In about 30 odd years Apollo Global team has grown to more than 1,700 employees and 550 investments professionals.

The firm has about $414 billion in assets under management, and Ares Management has more than $179 billion as of this writing.

Back in 2015, the couple appeared for the first time on the billionaire’s list with a total net worth of $1 billion which increased in 2017 to $1.8 billion.

As of this writing, the couple is worth more than $3 billion and change.

They are consistently placed among the most generous celebrities globally and have at times been the number 1 donor to charity out of any celebrity in the world.

Early Life

Jami Gertz was born on October the 28th, 1965, in Chicago, Illinois, United States. Jami was raised in a conservative Jewish family in the suburb of Glenview.

Her father, Walter Gertz, was a contractor and her mother, Sharyn, was a builder. She lived with her two brothers Michael and Scott Gertz, in Glenview.

Jami went to the public school and attended Maine East High School, and graduated in 1983.

Jami Gertz Charity

Jami Gertz has been known for her act of giving back along with her acting.

She has been actively involved in numerous charitable foundations and supports many causes.

In 2010, The Giving Back Fund listed Gertz and her husband Tony as the number-one donor to the charity of any celebrity.

She works as an active philanthropist and donates a huge chunk of money to a different charitable trust every year.

Jami, along with her husband Tony, established Ressler-Gertz Foundation in 1997 in Encino, California, with the motive of providing money to people in need.

The foundation has no employees, and the latest revenue was over $7,288,499.

Moreover, the duo also became a member of the Painted Turtle Camp, an organization that pays for children suffering from chronic and fatal illnesses on camping trips.

Furthermore, Jami also serves as a board member of Melanoma Research Alliance, a non-profit organization that provides funds for melanoma research.

In October 2020, the couple’s foundation also provided $5 million for entrepreneurship at Herman J. Russell Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (RCIE).

Also, the Gertz-Ressler High Academy is a college named after the couple as a member of The Alliance for College-Ready Public Schools.

Gertz and her husband’s basketball team, Atlanta Hawks, raised funds for the Prostate Cancer Foundation. They donated $200,000 to the foundation recently, in April 2021.
