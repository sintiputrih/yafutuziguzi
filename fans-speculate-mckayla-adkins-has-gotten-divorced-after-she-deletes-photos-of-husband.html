Fans Speculate McKayla Adkins Has Gotten Divorced After She Deletes Photos Of Husband

January 2024 · 2 minute read

Trouble in paradise?

After all signs of ‘Unexpected’ star McKayla Adkins‘ husband have disappeared from her Instagram, fans are convinced that the two have filed for divorce.

Credit: TLC

An eagle-eyed fan on reddit was the first to notice something different about McKayla Adams‘ Twitter.

McKayla has recently deleted her wedding photos from her Instagram, as well as several photos of her husband.

Given this is the tell-tale social media sign of a split, it’s not surprising that fans immediately began to speculate that the two may have divorced.

“I noticed as well. She did have a “family nail day” pic still up a few days ago. I saw when she started that bartending job & has shifted her identity again, the baby/family content stopped. Maybe the hubby isn’t apart of it anymore? just speculation, no hate,” one fan wrote on the reddit thread.

Another wrote, “I hope not for her families sake. She has a 8 month old baby doesn’t she?”

However, another explanation soon surfaced in the comments – many believe that McKayla may be hiding her relationship because of her OnlyFans.

“If she still does a lot of only fans she may have just removed it as it’s bad for her brand,” one fan pointed out.

Credit: TLC

One things fans know for sure is that McKayla will not be having any more children.

Over the summer, she shared with fans via her Instagram story that she has gone through with her plans for sterilization.

“Sterilized,” McKayla captioned this sleepy-looking selfie. “Anesthesia has me exhausted.”

McKayla has been outspoken about reproductive choice, deriding the Supreme Court’s decision to overturn Roe v. Wade, which federally protected abortion access.

“You won’t catch me celebrating a day about ‘independence’ when people who have a uterus have no independence over their own bodies,” McKayla share on Independence Day, along with other Instagram posts condemning SCOTUS’s decision.
