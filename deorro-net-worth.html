Deorro | Career

Deorro started composing music and spinning records when he was 14 years old. He started making his own music at the age of 17.

Around 2010, he began releasing music under the pseudonym TON!C.

In 2013, he released “Yee” under Hardwell’s label, Revealed Recordings.

The single charted in Austria, Belgium, France, Germany, the Netherlands, and Switzerland.

In April 2014, he released the track “Five Hours.”

Similarly, he and rapper Chris Brown collaborated on a third edition of “Five Hours,” titled “Five More Hours,” released in March 2015.

Moreover, he collaborated with fellow DJs Steve Aoki and Diplo and released the single “Freak” on 18 March 2014.

On 17 May 2014, Deorro announced on his Twitter page he was going on a hiatus to focus more on his record label, Panda Funk.
