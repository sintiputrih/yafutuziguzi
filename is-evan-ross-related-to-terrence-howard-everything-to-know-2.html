Is Evan Ross Related To Terrence Howard? Everything To Know

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Evan Olav Ross-Naess is an American actor and musician who made his acting debut in the comedy-drama film ATL in 2006. In 2010, he made his television debut and joined the cast of The CW’s 90210.

Most notably, Evan appeared in The Hunger Games: Mockingjay Part 1 and Part 2 in the role of Messalla. He is also known for his marriage to Ashlee Simpson with whom together they starred in six episodes of E!’s Ashlee + Evan reality TV series in 2018.

The same year in 2018, Evan and Ashlee released their first EP, Ashlee + Evan. 

Is Evan Ross Related to Terrence Howard?

Evan Ross and Terrence Howard are not related to each other. Even though on ‘Celebrity Family Fued’, Terrence introduced Evan as his nephew. Terrence is only a close family friend to Evan’s family.

Evan and Terrence are both American actors whereas Evan is a musician too. Evan made his acting debut in the comedy-drama film ATL (2006, and has since starred in the films Pride (2007), According to Greta (2009), Mooz-Ium (2010), 96 Minutes (2011), Supremacy (2014), and The Hunger Games: Mockingjay- Part 1 (2004) and Part 2 (2015).

However, Terrence had his first major role in the 1995 films Dead Presidents and Mr. Holland’s Opus. He broke into the mainstream with a succession of television and cinema roles between 2004 and 2006. He was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Actor for his role in Hustle & Flow.

Who is Evan Ross’s Family?

Evan Olav Ross-Naess was born on August 26, 1998, in Greenwich, Connecticut, U.S. He is the son of legendary music superstar Diana Ross, former lead singer of the Supremes, and late mountaineer Arne Næss Jr.

While Evan’s mother is African-American, his father is of Norwegian and German descent. He has an older brother, three older maternal half-sisters, three older paternal half-siblings, and two younger paternal half-siblings.

Evan is a married man. He became engaged to singer-songwriter Ashlee Simpson in January 2014, and the two married on his mother’s estate in Connecticut on August 30, 2014.

Evan and Ashlee have two children together, a daughter named Jagger Snow Ross( born July 30, 2015) and a son named Ziggy Blu Ross (born October 29, 2020), and Ross is the step-father of Bronx Mowgli Wentz ( born November 20, 2008), Simpson’s son by her previous husband, musician Pete Wentz.

Evan is very active on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. He has more than 185k followers on Facebook, more than 716k followers on Instagram with the user name @realevanross, and 48.8k followers on Twitter.
