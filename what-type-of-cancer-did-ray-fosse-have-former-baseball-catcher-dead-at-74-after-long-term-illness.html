What type of cancer did Ray Fosse have? The former MLB player and commentator dies after 16 years of battle against cancer.

Ray Fosse was an American professional baseball player and television sports commentator.

During his time as a player, Fosse was best known for playing for Cleveland Indians and Oakland Athletics. He played in the MLB for well over a decade.

Besides his gameplay as a player, people also loved him for his commentary since he was doing it for a couple of decades as well.

It was earlier this year that Ray stepped down from the broadcasting duties by revealing his cancer battle for the last 16 years.

Now, CBS Sports as well as all other sources confirm his demise at the age of 74 from same cancer.
