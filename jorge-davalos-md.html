Overview of Dr. Davalos

Dr. Jorge Davalos is a cardiologist in Colorado Springs, CO and is affiliated with multiple hospitals in the area, including University of Colorado Hospital, UCHealth Memorial Hospital, and UCHealth Poudre Valley Hospital. He received his medical degree from University of Miami Leonard M. Miller School of Medicine and has been in practice 15 years. Dr. Davalos accepts several types of health insurance, listed below. He is one of 100 doctors at UCHealth Memorial Hospital and one of 79 doctors at UCHealth Poudre Valley Hospital who specialize in Cardiology.
