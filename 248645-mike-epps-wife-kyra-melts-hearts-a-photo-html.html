Mike Epps' Wife Kyra Melts Hearts with a Photo Showing Their Daughter Indiana's Cute Wavy Hair

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Indiana Epps, the baby daughter of comedian Mike Epps and his wife, Kyra, recently stole the hearts of her mother's fans and followers after her doting mom shared a cute snap of her reading a book.

Comedian Mike Epps' wife, Kyra, recently caught her fans and followers off guard with the most precious and breathtaking snap of her baby daughter playing. The doting mom shared how much she misses her daughter and wrote:

"She's been sleep [sic)] for 35 minutes. I miss her already."

Mike Epps and Kyra Robinson attend the LA premiere of Netflix's "Dolemite Is My Name" on September 28, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

Finding that the loving mother missed her baby girl dearly even though she was with her but fast asleep must have been very heart-warming for her fans and followers.

The picture Kyra shared was a beautiful one of Indiana sitting on a bed while looking engrossed in a book. The adorable princess was dressed in a cute plum-colored pajama set.

To fight off the cold and keep her warm, little Indiana had her tiny feet tucked safely into a pair of black socks. Around her neck was a dainty gold necklace while she showed off her beautiful dark curly hair.

As she sat pretty in her cute outfit, Indiana stared intently at the large book in front of her, which had several fun-looking characters drawn on it. After all that learning, perhaps her nap was much needed.

She is not Mike's first child but his fifth.

Mike Epp's daughter, Indiana, looking at a playbook in an adorable shot posted by her mom, Kyra Epps. | Photo: Instagram/kyraepps

Although little miss Indiana looks all grown up with her enjoying reading books and stealing hearts by the minute, the sleeping beauty has only been around for a little less than a year!

Her loving parents first revealed that they were expecting her in December 2019 through a post on Mike's Instagram page. The comedian shared a breathtaking maternity picture of his wife with a caption that said:

"God bless my beautiful wife and our new born baby on the way ❤️❤️????"

The mommy-to-be also shared the wonderful news on her own Instagram page, with the gorgeous maternity shot in her beautiful white leotard as she embraced her growing baby bump.

Although beautiful Indiana came as Kyra and Mike's first child together, she is not Mike's first child but his fifth. The comedian is also the proud father of four daughters from previous relationships.

He and Kyra have been married for almost two years as they tied the knot on June 23, 2019. Indiana is a lucky girl to have parents that love her very much and even miss her when she goes to sleep!
