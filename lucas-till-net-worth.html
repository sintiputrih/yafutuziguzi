Lucas Till Wiki Biography

Lucas Daniel Till was born on the 10th August 1990, in Fort Hood, Texas USA, of English, Swiss, German and Scottish descent. He is widely known as an American actor, who has appeared in “The Hannah Montana Movie”, “X-Men: First Class”, and “X-Men: Days of Future Past”. Beside that, he is also recognized for starring in the music video for Taylor Swift’s song called “You Belong With Me”. He became active professionally in 2003.

A young American celebrity, how rich is Lucas Till as of 2015? According to sources it has been estimated that Lucas Till’s overall net worth is $2 million. He has earned this wealth mostly thanks to his acting skills, portraying a great number of characters in the television and movies.

Lucas Till is the older son of John and Dana Till. With his seven years younger brother called Nick, he was raised in Atlanta, Georgia, where he attended Kell High School until 2008. At a very young age, his father and mother noticed that he was very talented in performing various characters and voices. As an 11-year-old boy, he started attending acting classes, and later his talents were spotted by Joy Previs, an Atlanta agent. Thanks to him, Till began starring in a number of advertisements. His net worth was established.

In the year 2003, he was cast in the main role of Harry Vanderbilt in the movie “The Adventures of Ociee Nash”, directed by Kristen McGary. Soon after this movie, he continued by successfully starring as the character Jay Graves in “Lightning Bug”, with Laura Prepon and Brett Harrison. His net worth started rising. After these roles, he appeared in movies such as “Walk the Line” (2005) playing young Jack Cash, the older brother of Johnny Cash, “Not Like Everyone Else” (2006) as Kyle Kenney, “Laid to Rest” (2009), in the role of a young store clerk. Beside these movies, he appeared as a guest-star in TV series’ too, such as “House and Medium” (2008) in the role of Simon, and “Medium” (2009) playing Adam Mankowitz. All have added consistently to Till’s net worth.

While he was at the high school and before graduating, in 2009 he went to audition for Disney’s movie named “Hannah Montana: The Movie”, where Miley Cyrus picked him for the lead role of Travis Brody, and by that step Till’s career in the entertainment industry really started. This role was a major appearance for him, which led him to move to Los Angeles, California, to continue his career as an actor. For this role, he was nominated in 2009 for Teen Choice Awards twice – for Choice Movie Actor: Music/Dance and Choice Movie Liplock.

Some of Till’s most notable roles came after this Disney movie. He worked together with Jackie Chan in the movie “The Spy Next Door”, in which he played the role of a spy from Russia. Later, he starred in Taylor Swift’s music video for her song: You Belong to Me”. Besides that, he appeared in one episode of “House” and in the TV show called “Leo Little’s Big Show”. Except that, he plays the character Havok in the movies “X-Men: First Class”, which led him to nomination with Teen Choice Awards for Choice Movie Chemistry, and “X-Men: Days of Future Past”, directed by Matthew Vaughn. He will also be returning in the 2016 movie “X-Men: Apocalypse”. Recently, in 2015 he was cast in the main role portraying Josh Harvest in the movie “Bravetown”.

In addition to his acting career, Lucas Till has also worked as a movie producer for two movies. The first was called “All Superheroes Must Die” (2011), and the second one named “Wet and Reckless” (2013), which added a considerable amount to his net worth.

Regarding his personal life, so far Lucas Till remains single, and dedicated to acting since his first appearance in front of the camera.
