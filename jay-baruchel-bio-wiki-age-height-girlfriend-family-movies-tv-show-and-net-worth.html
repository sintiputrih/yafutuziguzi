    Career

    Jay rose to fame for his roles in How To Train Your Dragon, Tropic Thunder, Knocked Up, The Trotsky Fanboys among others. However, he made his acting debut at the age of 12. In 1996, his first major role was in a local TV series called My Hometown. He then co-hosted a 1997 kids show known as Popular Mechanics with Elisha Cuthbert.

    Later, Jay bagged the role of Steven Karp on Judd Apatow’s acclaimed TV series titled Undeclared. He went on to play another role in Dollar baby back in 2004 and was able to star in Nemesis Game and Fetching Cody in 2005. In 2006, Jay was chosen to guest star on a CBS drama called Numb3rs.

    His name grew more and more popular as he continued to star in various movies and TV shows in the film industry. His most recent roles are in films like Goon: Last of the Enforcers (2017) amd How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World (2019).
