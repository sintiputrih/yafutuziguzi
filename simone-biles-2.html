Career

Simone Biles started her career as a professional gymnast in the year 2011 with her participation in the American Classic in Houston where she was placed third in all-around, first in balance beam and vault, fourth in floor exercise, and eighth in uneven bars events. In the same year, she participated in the US Classic in Chicago where she was placed 20th in all-around events.

In the year 2012, she participated in the American Classic held in Texas where she was placed first in the all-around event. In the same year, she also competes at the USA Gymnastics National Championship, US Classic in Chicago, US National Championships in Missouri, and was named to the US Junior National Team. In the year 2013, she made her senior international debut with her participation in the American Cup, a FIG World Cup Event.

In the same year, she has participated in several national and international events like the City of Jesolo Trophy in Italy, US Classic, USA Gymnastics National Championships, World Artistic Gymnastics Championships in Belgium, and was selected for the Senior National Team. 

In the year 2014, she participated in the US Classic in Chicago after missing two events AT&T American Cup and Pacific Rim Championships due to injury.
